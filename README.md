This project uses SQL to solve real-world business problems across sales, marketing, customer retention, and product analysis. Key insights include identifying top customers by revenue, evaluating marketing campaign ROI, detecting inactive customers, and analyzing product categories by profit.

## Business Problems (Stakeholder Requests)
## Problem 1: 
Stakeholder: VP of Sales 
Question: "Who are our top 10 customers by total spend over the last 12 months, and what region are they from? We want to send them a VIP thank-you gift."

## Problem 2: Campaign ROI (Marketing Efficiency)
Stakeholder: Marketing Manager 
Question: "Which marketing campaign had the best Return on Investment (ROI)? Calculate the total revenue generated by each campaign versus its budget."

##  Problem 3: The "Ghost" Customers (Retention)
Stakeholder: Customer Success Lead 
Question: "Find all customers who signed up in 2025 but have never placed an order. We need to target them with a 'First Purchase' discount."

## Problem 4: Category Deep-Dive (Inventory/Sales)
Stakeholder: Product Manager 
Question: "Which product category has the highest profit margin? (Profit = Price - Cost of Goods)."
